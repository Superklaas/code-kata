ROMAN NUMERALS
I   V   X   L   C   D   M
1   5   10  50  100 500 1000

INPUT
arabic numeral as a string
- only digits in original string
- natural number: greater than 0
- smaller than 4000: from then on, other roman numerals are used which I won't consider in this exercise

SOLUTION WITH A LOT OF IF CLAUSES
sequence of divisions
/1000   M
/900    CM
/500    D
/400    CD
/100    C
/90     XC
/50     L
/40     XL
/10     X
/9      IX
/5      V
/4      IV
/1      I
